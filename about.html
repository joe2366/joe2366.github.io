Who We Are

Rainbow Whisker Kitten is a small, loving home-based cattery dedicated to raising healthy, happy, and well-socialized kittens. Every kitten is treated as family from day one.

Our Mission

Our mission is to match each kitten with a caring forever home while maintaining the highest standards of health, cleanliness, and ethical breeding.
