Frequently Asked Questions

Are the kittens on your website real?

Yes. All photos and videos shown are of kittens raised in our care. Live video calls can be requested for serious adopters.

Where are you located?

Our kittens are raised in a private home environment. For health and safety, visits are by appointment only.

Do you offer shipping or delivery?

Delivery options may be discussed depending on location. We prioritize kitten safety and comfort at all times.

What payments do you accept?

We accept secure reservation payments through approved methods such as PayPal or CashApp. We never request unsafe or anonymous payment methods.

Can I speak to someone before reserving?

Absolutely. You may contact us via email or live chat to ask questions before making any commitment.
